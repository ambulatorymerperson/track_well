if you just want to record weather or not you did something, call the unit 'y/n', for yes/no, and we will store it as a boolean type. For custom variables, try to choose behaviors that you do regularly or want to do regularly, rather than activities which you only do intermittently (for example, it would be better to track how often you walk dogs at your local shelter rather than how often you donate blood, since you can only donate blood once every two months). Additionally, when naming your variables, try to stick to verbs (for example "run" instead of "miles ran").
